How we work

Our Values

Open Science

We pre-register studies where appropriate, share data and code publicly, and aim to publish open access. We believe science is stronger when it's reproducible and transparent.

Mentorship First

Regular one-to-ones, structured career development conversations, and active support for fellowship and grant applications. We invest in you beyond the project.

Wellbeing Matters

We take mental health and work–life balance seriously. Research is a marathon, not a sprint. Flexible working, realistic expectations, and a supportive lab culture are non-negotiable.

Diversity & Inclusion

We actively welcome applications from candidates of all backgrounds, nationalities, and career histories. Diverse perspectives make better science — this is a genuine commitment, not a slogan.

Clinical Impact

Our science is grounded in real patient populations and clinical questions. You will have the opportunity to engage with NHS clinical partners and see your research connect to healthcare.

International Network

Active collaborations with groups in Europe, North America, and beyond. We support conference attendance and visiting fellowships to help you build your own professional network.

What to expect

Application Process

01

Informal Enquiry

Email us with your CV and a short paragraph on why you're interested. We respond to all enquiries.

02

Informal Call

A 30-minute video call to discuss the project, your background, and any questions you have about life in the group.

03

Formal Application

Submit through the University of Birmingham portal or funding body as required.

04

Interview

A formal interview, usually including a short presentation. We aim to give feedback to all candidates.

05

Offer & Start

We support you through onboarding, including introductions to collaborators and lab induction.

Get in touch

Interested? Talk to us first.

We strongly encourage informal enquiries before any formal application. It gives us both a chance to find out if the project and environment are a good fit.

There are no silly questions. We'd rather hear from ten curious people than miss one excellent candidate who didn't reach out.
